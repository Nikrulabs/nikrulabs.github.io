Meet Dr. Lakshmi Yarram: The Vision Behind Nikrulabs

Blending Passion, Expertise, and Family Values.

Nikrulabs is the innovative brainchild and creative outlet of **Dr. Lakshmi Yarram**. A proud mom of two, she built Nikrulabs as a passionate side pursuit that grew from her extensive **25+ year career in IT**. Holding a doctorate degree and a postdoctoral fellowship from Lehigh University in the USA, Dr. Lakshmi blends rigorous academic acumen with practical industry leadership.

Nikrulabs is where her profound expertise in data analysis, sustainable technology, and intuitive UI/UX design converges, creating impactful digital experiences rooted in both intellect and heart. It's a testament to how deep industry experience, academic rigor, and personal passion can cultivate meaningful digital solutions for a better tomorrow.
